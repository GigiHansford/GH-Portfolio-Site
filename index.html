Background

When I was a little girl, I used to drive my parents mad by painting my shoes different colors, because I didn't like to be the same as Sarah from next door or Jenny down the road. I'd paint murals on my walls and on my cupboard doors and on my wooden floor, you know, just to brighten them up. And I'd fastidiously categorise my wardrobe by item and then by colour, because you know it makes sense.

25 years later and I'm still that maverick, borderline-OCD creative-type, except these days -- to my parents' delight -- I prefer my muse to be other people's websites, rather than my bedroom furniture.
